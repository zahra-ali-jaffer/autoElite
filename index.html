About AutoElite Concierge

AutoElite Concierge is a luxury automotive service company based in the UAE, created for individuals who value time, convenience, and trust. Whether you drive a Toyota or a Rolls-Royce, we offer a seamless, stress-free experience by managing everything your car needs—so you don’t have to.


From regular maintenance and mechanical repairs to renewals, detailing, and RTA paperwork—we take care of it all. You hand us the keys, and we handle the rest.


Our curated network of trusted garages and service providers ensures your vehicle is in the best hands at all times, while you focus on what matters most.

At AutoElite, your time is the true luxury.
